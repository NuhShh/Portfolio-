### 1. E-Commerce Funnel Analysis for Maven Fuzzy Factory

**Overview:**  
Maven Fuzzy Factory, an e-commerce toy store, needed to understand where users were dropping off in their online purchase journey. I performed funnel analysis using SQL to extract and transform data from multiple tables, then built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize user flow and identify conversion bottlenecks.

**Goal:**  
Identify where users drop off in the purchase funnel and provide actionable recommendations to increase conversion rates and revenue.

**Tools/Skills:**  
SQL, Power BI, SQLite, Data Analysis, Data Visualization

**Result:**  
Analysis of 140,571 sessions revealed an 8.06% overall conversion rate with the biggest bottleneck at the "Add to Cart" stage (61.1% drop-off). A significant mobile vs desktop conversion gap was identified—desktop converts at 10.05% while mobile only reaches 3.45%. Additionally, two products showed alarmingly high refund rates exceeding 34%.  
Recommendations include implementing cart abandonment recovery, optimizing mobile UX, and auditing high-refund product quality.

### 2. Sales Performance & Product Profitability Analysis for Velox Cycles Europe

**Overview:**  
Velox Cycles Europe, a premium bicycle retailer, needed to understand where profit opportunities were being missed. I conducted sales and profitability analysis using Power BI and Excel to evaluate geographic performance and identify margin optimization opportunities.

**Goal:**  
Identify revenue concentration risks and cross-sell opportunities to improve overall profitability and market diversification.

**Tools/Skills:**  
Power BI, DAX, Excel, Data Modeling, Data Visualization

**Result:**  
Analysis of €85.27M in revenue revealed that 58% of revenue comes from just two markets (US and Australia), creating significant business dependency risk. Additionally, high-margin accessories (60–72% margin) are underutilized, contributing only €15M compared to €62M from bikes.  
Recommendations include implementing bundle strategies and expanding into underpenetrated markets to improve profitability.

[**See full project →**](https://github.com/NuhShh/Velox-Cycles-Profit-Analysis)
